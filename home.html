Welcome to the next generation of Xbox

Welcome to a concept look at the next generation of Xbox: a system built on two powerful ideas.

  • Hybrid Mode for the future of gaming
  • Legacy Mode for perfect backwards compatibility

Third‑Party Storefront Integration

Xbox Core X|S support a flexible ecosystem of digital storefronts through Hybrid Mode, letting players access stores like Epic, Steam, and EA alongside the Microsoft Store. Purchases stay secure with system‑level entitlement management, and all games appear in one unified library.

Legacy Mode ensures full compatibility with past Xbox titles, so that you are able to play your Xbox, Xbox 360, and Xbox One games.
